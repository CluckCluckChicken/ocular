privacy

ocular uses a number of services to provide various tools to enhance the experience. these services may collect and or use data according to their privacy policies.

this site uses a self-hosted instance of plausible analytics to track website usage. you can find out what kind of information is collected at the plausible analytics website.

for hosting, this site uses vercel and cloudflare.

this site also uses jsdelivr to serve some libraries used to make ocular work.

the data presented to users comes from scratchdb (forum post data and APIs) and fluffyscratch (profile picture by username api)

for external services, please consult their sites for information about how data is stored and used, if you have any questions about your privacy when using ocular feel free to contact me and ask about it.
